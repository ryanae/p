About

Hi, I'm Audrey. I'm a graduating senior at University of California, Irvine. I'm studying Informatics, specializing in Health Informatics, and minoring in Biology. I am currently residing in the Orange County for school, but I live in San Francisco.

So you're wondering, how does a health student find their way into Software and UX? I started with biology, then became interested in computer science. I didn't want to pick one or the other, so I picked an intersection of both fields: Health Informatics. I eventually discovered my affinity for Software and UX design because I love creating things. Also, it was learning that the time I've spent messing with CSS, HTML, and Photoshop wasn't a complete waste.

I love art and video games. As a creator, I love being able to craft something beautiful, and to tell a story for the world to see and hear. I, of course, love being part of the audience too. Overwatch, Cuphead, and Bloodborne are my current favorite games. I am hoping to break into the video game industry at some point in my life!

I'm a homebody—most of the time. I'm usually playing video games or lazing around with my dogs outside of school. But I also enjoy exploring Southern California—chasing food trends, going to the beach, and going to Disneyland!
