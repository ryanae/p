About


My name is Audrey! I’m currently a senior at University of California, Irvine. I’m studying Informatics, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction.

I sort of fell into UX. I always knew that I could be happy in any career as long as my work revolved around creating things. After shuffling around majors in college, I eventually discovered UX, and realized that it was the perfect intersection between creativity, problem solving, and technical craft.

I consider myself a fledgling designer. Aside from finishing my degree, I am focused on gaining more professional experience in UX as well as starting personal projects!

Messenger Redux

Facebook's Messenger app is one of the most popular and widely used messaging apps out there. The purpose of this redesign was to improve Messenger's overall user experience, as it has evolved into a cluttered app with many features that are unpopular and unnecessary.


Goals

  • Improve user experience by making app easier to use and navigate, refocus functionality to revolve around messaging
  • Declutter app by stripping away unpopular features
  • Redesign central navigation to create make certain features more apparent and accessible

Role & Duties

  • Project leader
  • Gather and document requirements
  • Competitor analysis
  • User interviews, user testing
  • UML, Mock-ups
  • Gather, analyze, document, and present interview and test data
  • Author final proposal
