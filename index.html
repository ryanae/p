About


My name is Audrey! I’m currently a senior at University of California, Irvine. I’m studying Informatics, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction.

Messenger Redux

Facebook's Messenger app is one of the most popular and widely used messaging apps out there. The purpose of this redesign was to improve Messenger's overall user experience, as it has evolved into a cluttered app with many features that are unpopular and unnecessary.


Goals

  • Improve user experience by making app easier to use and navigate, refocus functionality to revolve around messaging
  • Declutter app by stripping away unpopular features
  • Redesign central navigation to create make certain features more apparent and accessible

Role & Duties

  • Project leader
  • Gather and document requirements
  • Competitor analysis
  • User interviews, user testing
  • UML, Mock-ups
  • Gather, analyze, document, and present interview and test data
  • Author final proposal

Anteaters Stop Hunger

Anteaters Stop Hunger was a software project born from the desire to help reduce hunger and food waste in the area around University of California, Irvine. Specifically, the application aimed to connect UCI's eateries to local food shelters, and facilitate communication and deliveries between the two parties.


Goals

  • Create three different front-ends for the three users of the system: UCI staff, food shelter staff, drivers
  • Facilitate communication; allow UCI staff to post available food, allow food shelter staff to make requests, schedule deliveries
  • Provide users with live, accurate GPS tracking of deliveries
  • Ensure exchange and transportation of food to adheres food safety standards and regulations
  • Log history of reciepts, donations and other necessary documentation for tax purposes

Role & Duties

  • Gather requirements
  • Author documentation
  • Write case scenarios, use cases
  • UML, class diagrams
  • Stakeholder, goal models
